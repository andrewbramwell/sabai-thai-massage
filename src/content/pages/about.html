About Sabai Thai Massage & Spa

Sabai Thai Massage & Spa has been a haven of relaxation and healing in Bournemouth for four years. Our journey began with a passion for sharing the authentic healing traditions of Thailand with our local community.

Our story is deeply rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Thailand. Our founder, Bee hails from Buriram, a province known for its ancient Khmer temples and warm hospitality. In 2012, she brought her expertise and passion for Thai massage to the UK, driven by a vision to introduce this transformative therapy to the Bournemouth community.

Bee's journey is one of dedication and love for her craft. With recognised Thai massage qualifications, she combines traditional techniques with a deep understanding of modern wellness needs. Her goal is simple yet profound: to help people find relaxation and balance in their busy lives through the power of Thai massage.

As we continue to grow, our core values remain the same. We're committed to providing an authentic Thai massage experience, helping our clients achieve relaxation, and promoting overall wellness in our community.
